Exploring Zamboanga: Pasonanca Park

For our first adventure, we're going to my hometown: Zamboanga City!

It may seem pretty weird to call a leisurely stroll at home an "adventure", but this place has quite a few surprises!

Here's a place I still haven't been to, but really should've: Recognize this? It's the famous treehouse! That's right, I'm at Pasonanca Park! Despite my years living here in the city, only now can I call myself a real Zamboangueño, because I've finally been to this park everyone in Z.C. loves!

This park has a lot of attractions, so here's my tips for the stroll:

  • It's only PHP 5 to enter the Butterfly Garden!
  • In some days, visiting the santuary is usually free!
  • The food right in front of the Butterfly Garden is both cheap and good!
  • See more in Travel Tips!
